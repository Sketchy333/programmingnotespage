Extra section Tags

Here are some extra section tags to help organize your code more easily.

  • HEADER (used for the top portion of a page)
  • FOOTER (used for the bottom portion of a page)
  • NAV (used for the navigation bar on the page)
  • SECTION (used for the middle portion of the page)
  • DIV (typically nested inside of the semantic tags listed above to create the desired layout)
